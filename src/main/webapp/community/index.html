Community

Get Help & Connect

The Apache Unomi community is friendly, open, and here to help. Pick the channel that works best for you.

Not sure where to start?

Here’s how most people get help with Apache Unomi.

1

Check the Docs

Start with the documentation — most common questions are answered there.

2

Ask the Community

Subscribe to the users@ mailing list and post your question. It’s the most active channel.

3

Chat on Slack

For quick questions, join #unomi on the ASF Slack (free account for anyone).

Monthly Meeting

The community meets monthly online with alternating times for different time zones. Current status and roadmap are discussed. Everyone is welcome!

JIRA Issue Tracker

Report bugs, request features, or browse known issues. Create an ASF JIRA account to submit tickets.
